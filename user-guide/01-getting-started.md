# Getting Started with CatalogNow

## What is CatalogNow?

CatalogNow is an AI-powered product catalog enrichment app for Shopify. It automatically generates product descriptions, categorizes products, analyzes images, and improves SEO — all powered by AI models through OpenRouter.

## Installation

1. Install CatalogNow from the Shopify App Store
2. The app will request permission to read and write your products
3. Once installed, you'll see CatalogNow in your Shopify admin sidebar

## First-Time Setup

### Step 1: Get an OpenRouter API Key

1. Go to [openrouter.ai](https://openrouter.ai) and create a free account
2. Click "Keys" in the sidebar
3. Click "Create Key"
4. Copy the key (starts with `sk-or-`)

**Free models are available immediately** — no credit card required.

### Step 2: Add Your Key to CatalogNow

1. In CatalogNow, go to **Settings**
2. Paste your OpenRouter API key
3. Click **Validate Key** to test it
4. Click **Save Key**

### Step 3: Sync Your Products

1. Go to **Products**
2. Click **Sync from Shopify**
3. Your products will be imported into CatalogNow

### Step 4: Start Enriching

1. Click on any product to open the AI workspace
2. Use the AI panel to generate descriptions, categorize, or run full enrichment
3. Review AI suggestions in the **Enrichment Queue**

## Dashboard

The dashboard shows:
- **Catalog Health Score** — percentage of products enriched by AI
- **Quick Actions** — shortcuts to common tasks
- **Recent Activity** — latest AI and human changes
